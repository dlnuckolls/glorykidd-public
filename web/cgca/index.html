- WHO WE ARE -

Our Mission

Cedar Grove Christian Academy exists to faithfully proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ to a lost and dying world, to equip and train students to think and live Biblically, and to prepare the next generation of Christian leaders.

Core Values

We are committed to delivering the ABC's necessary for our students to succeed:

A - Academic Rigor
B - Biblical World-view
C - Christ centered

Our Students

Cedar Grove Christian Academy admits students of any race, color, and national or ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. It does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, or national or ethnic origin in the administration of its educational policies, admissions policies, scholarship programs, and athletic or other school-administered programs.
