We Are missions

We at Cedar Grove are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission found in Matthew 28. 

Matthew 28:19 states:  “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit."  

Annually we take up offerings to support mission organizations.  In December we collect money for International Missionaries with the International Mission Board through their Lottie Moon Offering.  We also commit to supporting other missionaries through monthly donations.

Embrace Grace

Having an unplanned pregnancy or being a young, single mom can be a time of mixed and bittersweet emotions but know you’re not alone. Embrace Grace is a support group designed to help and encourage young, single women that are experiencing an unplanned pregnancy. This support group provides spiritual and emotional support through the local church.

By joining an Embrace Grace group, you will have the opportunity to meet other moms who are in a similar life season, and mentors that have walked through similar situations to what you are now going through. Embrace Grace was created to help encourage, educate and empower young, single moms just like you, and to make sure that you know that you are loved and valued by God and the leaders at Embrace Grace.

Men on Mission

The Men’s Ministry at Cedar Grove exists foremost to glorify God. We do this by fostering community with one another, and by God’s grace, we glorify Him by living that out in our daily lives. 

All nations whom You made will come and worship before You, Lord, and they will glorify Your name. 
Psalm 86:9 

We gather for breakfast on the third Saturday of each month at 8:00 am. We eat breakfast together, have a devotional, and pray.We tackle projects around the church grounds, take care of the needs at homes of older church members and persons in the community. We have a great time of fellowship working together.

Womens Ministry

The vision of Cedar Grove’s Women’s Ministry is to encourage women to love the Lord our God with all their heart, soul, mind and strength; and to love our neighbors as ourselves. We endeavor to do so by encouraging women to worship God through a lifestyle of fellowship, prayer, Bible study, and service. We believe that as women grow and mature in godliness, they are better equipped to nurture and serve in their families, to enrich the church, and to evangelize our local communities and the world.  

Charm is deceitful, and beauty is vain, but a woman who fears the Lord is to be praised.  
Proverbs 31:30

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry is for students in 6th-12th grade. In our ministry to youth we are serious about presenting the reality of who God is as revealed in the Bible. We study God’s word to learn about God’s character, what He has done, what He is doing, and how we should respond to Him. We are intentional about sharing the Gospel and discipling youth so they grow and mature in their personal walk with Jesus. We want to see youth encounter the love of God, be encouraged, transformed, and equipped to impact the world for God’s glory.

Children's Ministry

WELCOME TO CG KIDS!!

  Our mission is to partner with parents to guide children towards developing a heart for God. 
   On Sunday mornings we have Children’s Worship for ages 3 – Second grade, teaching the children to worship through song, Bible study and prayer using Truth78 curriculum. 
   On Wednesday evenings children ages 3 years – 5th grade meet to study God’s Word using the Gospel Project curriculum. This curriculum is a chronological, Christ-centered  Bible study focused on God’s plan of redemption as revealed in Genesis through Revelation. It shows how all Scripture gives testimony to Jesus Christ.
