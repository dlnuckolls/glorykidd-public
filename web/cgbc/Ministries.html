We Are missions

We at Cedar Grove are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission found in Matthew 28. 

Matthew 28:19 states:  “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit."  

Annually we take up offerings to support mission organizations.  In December we collect money for International Missionaries with the International Mission Board through their Lottie Moon Offering.  We also commit to supporting other missionaries through monthly donations.

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry is for students in 6th-12th grade. In our ministry to youth we are serious about presenting the reality of who God is as revealed in the Bible. We study God’s word to learn about God’s character, what He has done, what He is doing, and how we should respond to Him. We are intentional about sharing the Gospel and discipling youth so they grow and mature in their personal walk with Jesus. We want to see youth encounter the love of God, be encouraged, transformed, and equipped to impact the world for God’s glory.

Children's Ministry

WELCOME TO CG KIDS!!

  Our mission is to partner with parents to guide children towards developing a heart for God. 
   On Sunday mornings we have Children’s Worship for ages 3 – Second grade, teaching the children to worship through song, Bible study and prayer using Truth78 curriculum. 
   On Wednesday evenings children ages 3 years – 5th grade meet to study God’s Word using the Gospel Project curriculum. This curriculum is a chronological, Christ-centered  Bible study focused on God’s plan of redemption as revealed in Genesis through Revelation. It shows how all Scripture gives testimony to Jesus Christ.
