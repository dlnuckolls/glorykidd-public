Church In Dialog

The Church in Dialogue seeks Christian unity without glossing over differences nor dispensing with truth.

Download Brochure 

The Church in Dialog?

TCID is a series of panel discussions and joint worship services between all orthodox Christian denominations that hold to the authority of Scripture and adhere to the fundamental tenets of the historic Christian faith.

To contribute to the theological literacy of all those who attend its installments.

To create more opportunities for Christians to recognize, pursue, and enjoy unity as members of Christ’ s body.

To give honor to God and declare His glory are the ultimate reasons for which this event series was founded.

The Gospel at work

May we be one; even as the Father is in Christ and Christ in us, that we may also be in the triune God, so that the world may believe that the Father sent Christ into the world to save us from sin and death.
