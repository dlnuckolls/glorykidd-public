Women's Ministry @ CGBC

The vision of Cedar Grove’s Women’s Ministry is to encourage women to love the Lord our God with all their heart, soul, mind and strength; and to love our neighbors as ourselves. We endeavor to do so by encouraging women to worship God through a lifestyle of fellowship, prayer, Bible study, and service. We believe that as women grow and mature in godliness, they are better equipped to nurture and serve in their families, to enrich the church, and to evangelize our local communities and the world.

Embrace Grace

Having an unplanned pregnancy or being a young, single mom can be a time of mixed and bittersweet emotions but know you’re not alone. Embrace Grace is a support group designed to help and encourage young, single women that are experiencing an unplanned pregnancy. This support group provides spiritual and emotional support through the local church.

By joining an Embrace Grace group, you will have the opportunity to meet other moms who are in a similar life season, and mentors that have walked through similar situations to what you are now going through. Embrace Grace was created to help encourage, educate and empower young, single moms just like you, and to make sure that you know that you are loved and valued by God and the leaders at Embrace Grace.
