Men on Mission

All nations whom You made will come and worship before You, Lord, and they will glorify Your name. 
Psalm 86:9 

The Men’s Ministry at Cedar Grove exists first and foremost to glorify God.

We do this by fostering community with one another, and by God’s grace, we glorify Him by living that out in our daily lives. 

We gather for breakfast on the third Saturday of each month at 8:00 am. We eat breakfast together, have a devotional, and pray.We tackle projects around the church grounds, take care of the needs at homes of older church members and persons in the community. We have a great time of fellowship working together.
